Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 06, 2026

At **tosns**, accessible from https://tosns.greenbee.cloud, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by tosns and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our service to connect your social media accounts (such as TikTok, YouTube, Instagram), we collect necessary OAuth tokens and profile information provided by the respective platforms to facilitate the content uploading process. We do not store your passwords.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website
  • Understand and analyze how you use our website
  • Facilitate the uploading of your content to connected social media platforms

3. Log Files

tosns follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your OAuth tokens and personal data. Access to your accounts is only used for the specific purpose of content management as requested by you.

5. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@greenbee.cloud.
